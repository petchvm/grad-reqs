Need proof you're graduating?
Interim certifications for employment
▼

If your employer needs proof of your upcoming graduation before the official documents (TOR, diploma, certificate of graduation) are released in October, the Registrar can issue interim certifications.

  1. Go to Window 1 and request a Service Request Form (or print your own: ↗ Service Request Form)
  2. Fill it out and request the certifications you need.
  3. Processing takes about 1 week (e.g., requested July 20 → available July 27).

Available now:

  • Certification of Completion of Prescribed Curriculum
  • Certification of Grades

Whether these suffice depends on your employer. More details to be added once the documents are received.
